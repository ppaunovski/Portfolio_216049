About me:

Who am I?

My name is Pavel Paunovski, and I'm a nineteen years old computer science undergraduate born and raised in a small country in South-Eastern Europe, called Macedonia.

Education

Recently I enrolled in undergraduate computer science studies at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje, Macedonia.
I graduated from the public high school Rade Jovchevski Korchagin, Skopje, with remarkable grades throughout the years.

Interests

I love learning new things, therefore I really enjoy coding, solving problems, puzzles and cross-words, also designing web pages etc.
Besides all the nerdy things I do, I love to travel, party, meet with people and make new friends, also I regularly work out. I appreciate a great movie or an exciting TV show, and love to listen to music, especially a great Rock'n'Roll piece.
